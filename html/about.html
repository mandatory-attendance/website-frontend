Kalaw Lagaw Ya Language

Kala Lagaw Ya is one of two traditional languages of the Torres Strait. Kala Lagaw Ya (also written as Kalaw Lagaw Ya) is the traditional language owned by the Western and Central islands of the Torres Strait. It is linguistically connected to the Aboriginal languages of the Australian mainland.
